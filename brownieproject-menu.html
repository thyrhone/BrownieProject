brownies

Traditional Brownie

Our traditional brownie covers the basics of a brownie and then some. Because our brownies are made with love, this is not just any kind of a traditional brownie. Every process that goes into it is well thought through, from the melting of the butter to the whisking. That's why it won Best Brownie of 2019.

Citrus Brownie

If you're feeling adventurous, discover why orange and chocolate go so well together. We are obsessed with the flavour over here at CB and we hope we can convert our customers too! Think of this brownie as the traditional brownie with bursts of flavour of orange and other citrus-y flavours. It's well worth it.

Hazelnut Brownie

Everyone knows the classic chocolate and hazelnut combination. Here at CB, we have taken it up a notch. Our Hazelnut Brownie fuses that nutty texture and taste with a smooth and rich chocolate brownie and has made it mouth-wateringly irresistable. That's why Dolly Parton had it and loved it too!

orange and chocolate milkshake

If you can keep an open-mind, try our orange and chocolate milkshake. The orange compliments the chocolate so well, and it's like a tropical milkshake! Willy Wonka would die if he tried this, no cap.

strawberry and banana milkshake

The classic strawberry milkshake has been a fav throughout the years. Our strawberry and banana milkshake is designed to go well with all of our brownies on offer. We find it makes ordering with more fun!

hazelnut and caramel milkshake

Our hazelnut and caramel milkshake is different and delicious! Every milkshake is made with love and care but this is a little more special. We make the caramel syrup ourselves and source the hazelnuts locally!
